Czehoski

Czehoski's rooftop patio is one to fill up fast, meaning the determined patio-seekers should head straight up to its second floor (and bypass the provocative art displayed on Czehoski's walls).

The Drake

For most Torontonians, the Drake is a bar with a hotel somewhere overhead, but it took a few years for the bar to develop its identity after the grand renovation and reopening seven years ago.

The Gladstone

There are few success stories like the Gladstone, whose painstaking reinvention from flophouse to destination was centred around the Melody Bar.

Gusto 101

Gusto 101 offers two patios; one at street level and one with a retractable glass ceiling on the rooftop.

Squirly's

Squirly's is a small bar on Queen near Niagara is a good bet for a cold pint and cozy conversation among friends. They have food too including a weekend brunch.
